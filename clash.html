About the Clash

Clash One brings two street-born movement cultures face to face. Litefeet out of Harlem (Phil Eezy), built in the subway, fast, reactive, rhythm-driven — and Dancehall out of Jamaica (Chuby Dice), built in the garrison, grounded, expressive, shaped by sound system culture.

Clash Inna Dancehall is not about which style is "better". Both come from pressure and community.

Clash reintroduces the contemporary street dance battle genre with Jamaican culture, leading with its genre-defining Soundclash tradition.

The Clash series focuses on building a thorough understanding of Dancehall vocabulary, through opportunities to battle & learn from specialist teachers across a range of street styles in Naarm, to create new synergies.

Follow on Instagram for announcements on dates, registration, and battle format.
